— Method

Built on three commitments.

— 01

Government sources only

Rental medians from NSW Fair Trading's Rental Bond Board. Sale prices from NSW Valuer General. No scraped listings, no estimated values — the actual transacted record.

— 02

One composite score

Investment score weights gross yield at sixty percent and affordability at forty. A single number that rewards both income and accessibility, without sales-agent gloss.

— 03

Updated each quarter

As NSW Government releases new quarterly data, PropLens incorporates it within forty-eight hours. The dashboard reflects today's market, not last year's.

— Provenance

The same data the Department uses.

Every figure in PropLens traces back to an official NSW Government dataset. You can audit any number, any quarter, against the original release. Nothing is modelled, nothing is implied — only published, validated, public-record property data, presented well.
